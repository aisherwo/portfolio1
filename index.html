About Me


Born and raised in Thunder Bay, Ontario, I have always been heavily inspired my art. By the age or 4, I was consistently drawing cartoon characters I saw on TV, before eventually trying out realism as I got older.
After graduating high school and pursuing an undergraduate degree in Psychology at Lakehead University, I decided it was time to try and pursue my lifelong dream of creating art for a living. I packed my bags and moved to BC for a change of scenery and hopefully some inspiration.
This move not only inspired my art as a whole, but drove me to pursue Graphic Design, and register for school again.
I came back to Thunder Bay and registered in the Interactive Media Development program. This program opened my eyes to a world of different forms of media. I now am passionate not only about graphic design, but 3D design and interactive development.
