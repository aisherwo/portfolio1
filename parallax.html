Project Description

A group of classmates and I worked together on a project called "Sinfonietta", which is an platformer RPG game. I developed the background, created with the parallax effect, to give the illusion of 3D for a 2D game. The trees were developed by Hayden Veillieux and edited by me to fit the scene.

Project Details

  • Created in Adobe Illustrator
  • Colour pallette chosen by Adam Foster
  • Completed project April 2019
